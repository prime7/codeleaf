## The Problem: After-Hours Inquiries Go Cold

If you run a trades business in Edmonton — plumbing, electrical, HVAC, roofing — you know the pattern. A homeowner discovers a burst pipe at 9 PM. They Google "emergency plumber Edmonton," find your website, and... nothing. No one answers the phone. The chatbot doesn't exist. They move to the next result.

**You're not losing to better tradespeople. You're losing to faster responders.**

## 1. 24/7 Instant Response (Even at 2 AM)

An AI chatbot on your website answers immediately — any time, any day. It can:
- Ask qualifying questions ("Is this an emergency or a quote?")
- Collect contact details
- Schedule appointments directly into your calendar
- Send you an SMS alert for urgent calls

### Real Example
An Edmonton HVAC company we worked with was missing 70% of after-hours inquiries. After implementing a chatbot, they captured **43% more leads** in the first month — without hiring overnight staff.

## 2. Automatic Quote Estimation

For non-emergency work, the chatbot can provide rough estimates based on:
- Square footage
- Type of service
- Urgency level

This filters out price shoppers and pre-qualifies serious buyers before you ever speak to them.

## 3. Smart Scheduling Integration

Connect the chatbot to your Google Calendar or Jobber account:
- Customer picks a time slot
- Booking gets added to your calendar
- Customer receives confirmation SMS
- You get notified via email or Slack

No more phone tag. No more "I'll call you back tomorrow."

## 4. Follow-Up Sequences

Not every inquiry converts immediately. The chatbot can:
- Send a follow-up email 24 hours later
- Text a reminder 3 days later
- Offer a seasonal maintenance promo 2 weeks later

This turns "maybe later" into booked appointments.

## 5. Multi-Channel Presence

Your chatbot shouldn't live only on your website. Deploy it to:
- **Facebook Messenger** — where homeowners message first
- **SMS/Text** — for customers who prefer texting
- **Google Business Profile** — direct messaging

## What This Costs

Unlike hiring a receptionist ($3,000+/month), an AI chatbot setup costs:
- **One-time build:** $1,500 – $3,500
- **Monthly maintenance:** $50 – $200

Most Edmonton trades businesses see ROI within 30 days.
