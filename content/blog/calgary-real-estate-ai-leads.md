## The 5-Minute Rule in Calgary Real Estate

Studies show that responding to a lead within 5 minutes increases conversion by **900%**. After 30 minutes? Your odds drop by 21x.

In Calgary's fast-moving market — where homes in Bridgeland, Beltline, and Kensington can get multiple offers in days — speed isn't a luxury. It's the difference between a commission and a missed opportunity.

## The AI Advantage: Always-On Lead Capture

### Scenario 1: The Late-Night Zillow Browser

It's 10:47 PM. A couple in Tuscany scrolls through listings on their phone. They see your listing on Zillow and click through to your website.

**Without AI:** They fill out a contact form. You get an email. You respond at 8 AM. They've already contacted 3 other agents.

**With AI:** The chatbot greets them instantly, asks about their budget and timeline, shows comparable listings, and books a showing for Saturday at 2 PM. You wake up to a confirmed appointment.

### Scenario 2: The Missed Call

A prospect calls while you're in a showing. It goes to voicemail.

**Without AI:** They don't leave a message. You call back 2 hours later. They went with another agent.

**With AI:** A voice agent answers immediately: "Hi, you've reached [Agent Name]'s assistant. I can book a showing, send listings, or take a message. What would you like to do?" The appointment is booked before you finish your current showing.

## Compliance: RECA and PIPEDA

Real estate in Alberta has specific compliance requirements:
- **RECA advertising guidelines** — all automated messages must identify the brokerage
- **PIPEDA** — client data handling must be disclosed
- **Do Not Contact** — automated sequences need easy opt-out
